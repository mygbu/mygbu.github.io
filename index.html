  • Syllabus
    • MA1010: Calculus 1 - Functions of One variable 

    Real Sequences - boundedness, convergence

    Numerical Series - Test of convergence, alternating series, absolute convergence.


    Differential calculus - Limit, continuity and differentiability of functions, properties of differentiable functions - Rolle's theorem, Mean value theorem, Taylor's formula, maxima, minima, points of inflection, asymptotes and curvature

    Integral Calculus - Definite integral as a limit of a sum, properties of definite integrals, applications of definite integrals.


    Sequences and series of real functions, power series and Fourier series.
